Viva la Vegan was birthed out of a need for individuals with dietary restrictions to easily identify vegan friendly menu options at restaurants we all know and love. Developed by a team of three WDI students, this is a two models app with a AngularJS front-end that runs on a Rails API backend. My primary role on this project was to incorporate the CRUD functionality that allows users to read, update, create, and delete restaurant chains.
